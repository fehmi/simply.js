Keana’s silvery smooth vocals flow over luscious chords and bubbling beats from Jamie Blake in this lakeside Push 3 performance.

Watch the duo break down each layer of their Set, and see how they mapped different parameters of the electric piano and lead guitar patches to Push’s pads for more expressive playing. Stick around to hear Live 12.1’s Auto Shift at work on Keana’s vocals, too.
